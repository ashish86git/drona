About Us

Drona Logitech is your trusted partner for comprehensive logistics solutions. We pride ourselves on offering end-to-end supply chain services, encompassing inplant services, storage, warehousing, inventory management, packaging solutions, and last-mile deliveries — all seamlessly integrated under one roof.

At Drona, we leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance efficiency, provide real-time tracking, and deliver data-driven insights. Our collaborative and strategic approach ensures tailored solutions that align with your unique business needs. With a global reach and local expertise, we navigate the intricacies of supply chain management, offering a seamless and reliable experience.

Mission

By 2027, our goal is to establish ourselves as the unequivocal and most trusted strategic logistic partner for our customers, entrusted with the seamless management and execution of comprehensive 4PL operations.

Vision

Leading the industry by providing customized, tailor-made logistic solutions that empower customers to optimize their costs while leveraging tech excellence, reinforcing a dedicated, digitized, and sustainable experience.
